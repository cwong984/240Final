ABOUT US

BECKENSTEIN FABRICS & INTERIORS is your source for thousands of fabulous fabrics, custom window treatments, superbly crafted upholstery and custom slipcovers. Let our design staff help you realize your vision whether you are reupholstering one chair or creating a design scheme for your home, office or retail space.

At Beckenstein Fabric & Interiors we provide everything you need under one roof! Our fabric and wall covering Library is filled with thousands of brilliant designs that make your projects happen. We reupholster or create new furniture; measure, construct and install custom window treatments and slipcovers; upholster walls; create all of your bedding needs and more. We pick-up and deliver too! We offer “Shop-at-Home” services, for your convenience. Our design staff will come to you to help coordinate your fabric choices that fit your lifestyle; whether it’s traditional, modern or transitional, we can help.
